All the mistakes I've made


Yeah first off, sorry for the eye strain due to the pathetic backgrounds and colors on this website. Also, I am a little too lazy to format the following paragraphs, so sorry.

Now that that's out of the way, let's dive straight into it. Mistake 1, using Comic Sans. Never making that ever again.

Mistake 2, making it a one page website. While I might have deceived you into thinking there are multiple pages into play, but there's only really one page, with everything scrammed in. This was highly unnecessary and makes changing stuff up much more effort consuming.

Mistake 3, hard coding everything. Maybe I should've dynamically worked with the projects and stuff because there's no way I am going to update them every time a new project/internship happens. That's right, your boy's grown to internships.
