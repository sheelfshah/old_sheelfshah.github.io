All about me


First off, I am a student pursuing electrical engineering in a dual degree programme at IIT Bombay. In case you wonder, my name is Sheel, I am 19, and I live in Mumbai.

Now that you've measured my ass, let me talk some more about myself. I am an avid reader and also somewhat of a fitness freak. I play decent badminton, and am always open to sports. I listen to a lot of music too, but never have I come across someone who appreciates my taste for electronic music, so I guess that it is my choice that's poor.

Academically, I enjoy math and computer science, but only for as long as it doesn't get too theoretical. Fun Fact - all the math that I have been coming across is way too theoretical, and whilst I feel intimidated at first, it doesn't turn out too bad and in fact, makes a lot of sense. But it's too geeky to admit that you enjoy such deep math, and so it'll take some time before I can go talking about it.

Fun Fact 2 - I enjoy watching PewDiePie, if you haven't already guessed. Also, for other coding addict mates, I am a Sublime Text fanatic and if you ever want to spark a debate with me, all you have to do is name another editor.

Let's go champ! (Yet another reference)

Ultimate Tic Tac Toe AI

An AI agent that plays the complex game of ultimate tic tac toe using the pruned minimax algorithm. A GUI that allows you to play against the bot has also been implemented.

Sentiment Analysis

A team project as a submission to the StudentCoder challenge by Hexaware. A tool that analyses product review sentiment and produces insights based on different categories such as quality, price, etc. We finished in the top 3.

ML Gym

A group project taken under SoC IIT-B that implements machine learning algorithms from scratch and allows users to train/test them on the website. I created all of the backend, most of the frontend, and implemented a couple of ML algorithms.
